Nepal Super league

The Nepal Super League, simply known as the NSL, is Nepal's first professional franchise club football league organised by the Nepal Sports and Events Management with technical support of the ANFA

Martyr's Memorial A division league

Martyr's Memorial A-Division League also known as the Qatar Airways Martyr's Memorial A-Division League for sponsorship reasons, is a professional football league in Nepal and the highest level of the Nepalese football league system

Martyr's Memorial B division league

Martyr's Memorial B-Division League is the second tier of the association football league of Nepal football league system after the Martyr's Memorial A-Division League.
